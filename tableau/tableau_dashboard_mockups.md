# Tableau Dashboard Mockups

## Executive KPI Dashboard

Layout:
- Top row: Total Revenue, Latest Monthly Growth %, Average Transaction Value, Total Orders.
- Middle row: Monthly revenue trend and monthly growth bar chart.
- Bottom row: Top products, top regions, and top sales channels.

Filters:
- Transaction year
- Region
- Category
- Sales channel

## Revenue Trend Dashboard

Layout:
- Full-width monthly revenue trend.
- Growth percentage below the trend to separate scale differences.
- Highest revenue months ranking table.

Recommended interactions:
- Click a month to filter downstream category, product, and region dashboards.
- Use year filter for executive review meetings.

## Product Performance Dashboard

Layout:
- Ranked product bar chart by `total_revenue`.
- Product contribution percentage table.
- Category selector to focus product rankings.

Recommended interactions:
- Highlight products with high revenue but lower unit volume for pricing analysis.
- Highlight products with high units but lower revenue for bundle or upsell planning.

## Category Dashboard

Layout:
- Category revenue comparison.
- Category share of revenue.
- Orders and average transaction value by category.

Recommended interactions:
- Use category selection as a dashboard action for product details.

## Regional Dashboard

Layout:
- Region ranking by revenue.
- Regional order and unit volume comparison.
- Regional contribution percentage.

Recommended interactions:
- Use region filter to compare channel mix and top product contribution.

## Sales Channel Dashboard

Layout:
- Channel revenue comparison.
- Channel order count and average transaction value.
- Revenue contribution percentage by channel.

Recommended interactions:
- Compare online and offline performance.
- Identify channels with high average transaction value but low order volume.
